Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Commitment to Privacy

CALCI Horology SA ("CALCI", "we", "our") respects the absolute privacy of our esteemed clientele. In accordance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) and international GDPR regulations, all client communications and transaction records are encrypted using 256-bit Swiss bank-grade security protocols.

2. Information We Collect

We collect essential information required to fulfill acquisitions, verify Swiss insured delivery, and maintain private registry records:

  • Full Name, Email Address, and Phone Number
  • Insured Delivery Address and Billing Specifications
  • Timepiece Serial Registration and Service History

3. Non-Disclosure & Confidentiality

CALCI will never sell, rent, or lease private client data to third parties. Data is accessible solely to authorized concierge staff for order fulfillment, insured shipping logistics, and warranty certification.

4. Client Rights & Inquiries

You reserve the right to request a complete transcript of your personal data held in our Geneva registry or request deletion. Please direct inquiries to privacy@calci.ch.
