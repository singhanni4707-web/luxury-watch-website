Handcrafted Precision

Founded in the heart of Geneva, Switzerland, CALCI operates on a singular philosophy: every timepiece is a mechanical symphony. Our master watchmakers assemble over 300 micro-components by hand, ensuring that each caliber achieves COSC-certified chronometer status.

From sapphire crystal cases to hand-engraved rotor plates, our creations represent an unbroken tradition of Swiss horological art.
